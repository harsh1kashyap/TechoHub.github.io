Dua Associates Internship 2021 - Eligibility, Careers, Salary, Experience

Dua Associates Internship 2021 - Eligibility, Careers, Salary, Experience

In this post we will discuss in detail about Dua Associates internship program and how to get an internship in their firm. If you are currently pursuing a degree in law or are a recent graduate, you should definitely apply to this opportunity. They are one of the best law companies in India and a perfect firm to start your career with. They are always looking for fresh interns to join their offices located in Delhi, Chennai, Chandigarh, Bangalore, Gurugram, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune.

Read on to find the detailed eligibility criteria, careers, reviews, application process, salary and internship experiences of various students.

About Dua Associates

Dua Associates was founded in 1986 and is one of India's most renowned national law companies, having full-fledged offices in Delhi, Bangalore, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Gurugram, Pune, Chennai, and Chandigarh. Many Fortune 500 corporations, publicly-listed and public-sector enterprises, privately-owned businesses, global and multilateral organisations, and major brand names from the US, Europe, Japan, and ASEAN continue to be among the firm's broad customer base.

Dua Associates has also played a key role in aiding a number of large international corporations in establishing a presence in India. It has established itself as a prominent and stable institution, with a practise that is known for its expertise and honesty.

It is also well-known for its in-depth knowledge of national and regional legislation and regulation. It has also formed specialist practise groups to focus on new and developing sensitive industries in order to stay up with the changing legal environment. Dua Associates' pan-India presence allows it to build dedicated teams in any of its locations to fulfil customer needs and deal with the complexities of complicated transactions.

Eligibility Criteria

  • 1. You must be a law student, pursuing a full time law degree from a government recognized law school in India.
  • 2. You must be keen to pursue a career in law and have decent academic grades.
  • 3. There are no other strict requirements laid down by the company.
NOTE: Although you can apply for the internship at any year of study, it is recommended to apply in 2nd year and above.

Stipend / Salary at Dua Associates

The internship at Dua Associates is unpaid. But, you will gain a lot of knowledge and hands on experience.

Also, you will get an internship certificate. Moreover, you may also get hired as a full time entry-level associate after the internship.

How to apply at Dua Associates?

Interested LLB students and BA.LLB students can apply for this internship. Simply, you can send an email to dua.hr@duaassociate. Make sure you attach your updated CV and cover letter in the email.

If you do not get any reply, you can also try sending a mail to Ms. Catherine Cardoza at cardoza@duaassociates.com. Try searching for people already working in Dua Associates on LinkedIn and approach them for an internship opportunity.

If none of the above methods work, simply fill the contact form on their official website or call at their office - 011 2371 4408 and ask for opportunities.You can expect a quick reply from their side regarding the acceptance or rejection of your application.

Your CV should be fully personalized and look impressive. It must contain your basic information such as candidate's name, year of college, college name phone number, email address, preferred internship period. Moreover make sure your CV stands out by adding your published articles, research papers, moot court experience, previous internship experience etc.

Dua Associates Internship Experience

Most of the interns had a very good experience working at Dua Associates. The timings are fixed - 9 am to 6 pm and weekends are off. The duration of internship is 1 month.

The work atmosphere is very comfortable and the staff including associates and workers are very helpful. The seniors are always there to guide the interns. You need to complete a variety of legal research work and you'll also get a chance to attend arbitration proceeding and client meetings.

The office has a big library and pantries, which is a plus point. All of the interns work inside the library. The work will be tiring and you might face some difficulty, but in the end its all worth the efforts.

Final Verdict

Hope now you have enough knowledge about Dua Associates Internship . Do apply for the same and encourage for friends to apply for this opportunities. Do share this article with your law buddies and visit Internshipslive for more law related career opportunities!
